# Template — journal entry

Copy the block below to the TOP of `journal.md` (newest first). Keep all three parts — an entry missing its lesson is half an entry (RULE 16's spirit applies to memory too).

---

```markdown
## #N — YYYY-MM-DD — <five-word title>

**What:** <What was done, concretely. PRs by number. Facts, not intentions.>

**Why:** <Why it was done — the decision or need behind it, in plain words.>

**Standing directives (only if the human issued new orders — remove this line otherwise):**
1. <Directive, with its "done when" stated.>

**One thing to take from it:** <The single lesson the human should keep. Connect it to THEIR project, not to abstractions.>

— <seat> seat<, audited by [seat]><, merged by the human>
```

---

**Rules of the file:** newest entry on top · every working session writes one · standing directives live in entries and stay in the live file until fulfilled or withdrawn · the journal is the memory — if it's not in here, the factory doesn't remember it.
