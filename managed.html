How managed works

One permission. Nothing else. Yours to revoke.

Once you are set up, a small SquidBay app runs on your factory, scoped to exactly one thing: opening pull requests. It cannot merge, and it cannot touch your main branch. The single permission stays yours to revoke in one click, anytime.

  1. 01

    You send a request

    Tell us what you need, in plain words. No tickets, no dashboards.

  2. 02

    We do the work

    The seats plan, build, and check each other, the same way your own factory runs.

  3. 03

    A pull request lands

    It arrives in your factory reading like a plain letter: what changed, and why.

  4. 04

    You merge

    Nothing ships until you click. The gate stays yours, always.

What we can never do

Merge anything, touch your main branch, or hold any of your other credentials. We are a contractor with a proposal pad. Your factory is yours.
